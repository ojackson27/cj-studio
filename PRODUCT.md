# Product

## Register

brand

## Users

UK small business owners (retail, hospitality, services, trades) who are evaluating whether to hire CJ Studio. They arrive with a brief or a vague need for "a better website." They're non-technical. They judge capability entirely by how the CJ Studio site looks and feels — if it impresses them, they assume the studio can do the same for their business.

## Product Purpose

CJ Studio is a two-person UK web design studio (Ollie Jackson + Josh Carter) that builds fast, modern websites for UK businesses at a flat fee with no agency markup. The site is simultaneously the studio's marketing vehicle and its live portfolio demo — it must perform at the level the studio promises to deliver for clients.

Success: a prospective client lands, is immediately impressed, and submits the contact form.

## Brand Personality

Performance-industrial. Premium-athletic. Apple-level restraint with earned bursts of aurora colour. Confident without being cold; precise without being sterile.

Three words: sharp, kinetic, assured.

## Anti-references

- Generic Squarespace / Wix UK agency templates: stock photos, blue buttons, grid-of-services layout, zero craft
- Big US SaaS dark mode (Webflow, Linear, Vercel): hero metrics, token stat rows, startup-product grammar — wrong register for a UK craft studio targeting local businesses
- The site must be demonstrably more engaging than either of these: every surface is proof of work

## Design Principles

1. **The site is the portfolio.** Every pixel argues "we can do this for you." Anything generic undermines the sale.
2. **Restraint earns the bursts.** Pure white voids and tight type make the aurora palette land harder. Colour isn't everywhere; it punctuates.
3. **Motion is the differentiator.** The animations are what a local business owner can't build themselves and can't get from a template. They must be purposeful and technically impressive, not decorative.
4. **Precision over personality.** Sharp tracking, exact spacing, zero unnecessary elements. Personality comes from the aurora system and motion, not from whimsy.
5. **Trust through transparency.** Flat fee, two named founders, 50/50 payment structure — the copy earns trust by being specific, not aspirational.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

WCAG 2.1 AA. Keyboard navigable. Reduced-motion alternatives on all animations. Contrast ratio ≥4.5:1 for body text, ≥3:1 for large/bold text. Screen reader support for all interactive elements.
