Glass styling per object

  • Triangular prism: SVG polygon with brand gradient fill at ~55% opacity, white inner cutout, white/60 stroke border
  • Tall rectangle: Lavender→sky blue gradient, backdrop-blur, white/55 border, subtle purple glow shadow
  • Sphere: Radial gradient (rose→lavender→sky), white/60 border, pink glow shadow
  • Small cube: Mint→sky gradient, backdrop-blur, white/55 border

Reduced motion fallback

All 4 objects render in their final positions immediately. Browser mock fully visible. No transforms applied. Zero JS overhead.
